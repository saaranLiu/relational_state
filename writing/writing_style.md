## 6. writing style
* 严格遵循学术写作规范，一切以事实和文献为依据，不能输出没有证据的内容。
* 不使用复杂句， Ensure every sentence has a clear purpose，不使用不必要的形容词和副词。
* * **Do not** use em-dashes (—) or en-dashes (–). Use commas or separate sentences.
* **Do not** use quotation marks (" ") and ** for emphasis.
* Maintain a formal, objective, and concise academic tone.
* Use explicit transition words
